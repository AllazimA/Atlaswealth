The Founder

Ahmed Allazim
Built on Presence, Driven by Excellence

Ahmed Allazim is a Dubai-based luxury lifestyle and private concierge specialist with over 15 years of hands-on experience serving HNWI and UHNWI clients across the Middle East and globally.

His career spans the world's most recognised names in luxury service — from Chief Concierge at Hilton Hotels across the GCC, to Lifestyle Manager at Quintessentially Dubai (the world's leading private members club), to Lifestyle Operations Manager at Aspire Lifestyles by International SOS where he personally established the Dubai branch and built a network of over 370 luxury providers across the GCC.

Later, as a Platinum Relationship Manager at American Express and Senior Relationship Manager at First Abu Dhabi Bank, Ahmed developed deep expertise in HNWI financial advisory — making him uniquely positioned at the intersection of wealth, lifestyle, and access.
